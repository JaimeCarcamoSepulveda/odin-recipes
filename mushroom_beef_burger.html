Before we get started you'll need the following ingredients...

    LIST OF INGREDIENTS NEEDED
  • two lbs of ground beef
  • one onion chopped --to perfection!
  • three garlic gloves minced
  • one tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • one teaspoon of salt
  • half a teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • butter

  1. Remove ground beef from refrigerator and let stand for 1/2 to 2 hours --the longer the better
  2. Mix mushrooms, onion, salt, garlic, ltalian seasoning, salt and pepper all on a large bowl
  3. form mixer into four 1/2 inch thick patties
  4. butter up an indoor grill pan
  5. cook patties until brown and no longer pink in the center
    note should be about ten minutes per side
  6. eat up and enjoy with a tall glass of german beer!
