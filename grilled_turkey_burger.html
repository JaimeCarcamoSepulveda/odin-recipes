Before we get started you'll need the following ingredients...

    LIST OF INGREDIENTS NEEDED
  • one lbs 93% lean ground turkey
  • two tablespoons chopped red onion
  • two tablespoons of sour cream
  • one tablespoon of dijon mustard
  • two tablespoons of olive oil
  • half a tablespoon of salt
  • four thin slices of swiss cheese
  • four hamburger buns

INSTRUCTIONS FOR PREPARATION

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and oil grate lightly
  2. combine turkey, red onion, sour cream, mustard and steak seasoning in a bowl until well mixed
  3. form into four 3/4 inch thick patties. Drizzle with oil, salt and season on both sides
  4. cook about four minutes per side until cooked. lay cheese for one minute before removing
  5. eat all four burgers and enjoy being a pig
  6. you just ate four burgers brah!
